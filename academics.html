Facilities

State-of-the-Art
Academic Infrastructure

Geology Petrology Lab

Equipped with high-precision polarizing microscopes, rock thin-section preparation tools, and an extensive collection of mineral and rock specimens for detailed petrographic analysis.

Computer Science Lab

High-speed workstations with Linux/Windows environments, configured with the latest developer toolchains for software programming, web design, and database administration.

Central Library & Archives

Houses over 50,000 reference volumes, subscription journals, and digital research databases (INFLIBNET, N-LIST) with automated cataloging systems.

Physics & Chemistry Labs

Fully stocked experimental apparatus for spectroscopy, electromagnetism study, digital electronics design, and chemical analysis, supporting both UG and research streams.
