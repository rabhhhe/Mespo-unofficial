Admissions

Begin Your
Academic Journey.

We welcome applications from students worldwide who display academic brilliance, creative capabilities, and a commitment to personal growth. Our transparent admission procedures follow merit criteria aligned with Calicut University schedules.

View Admission Guide ↓

Step-by-Step

How to
Apply

01

CAP Portal Registration

Register through the University of Calicut Centralised Admission Process (CAP) portal and choose MES Ponnani College as your preferred institution.

02

College Portal Submit

Submit your details and CAP registration number on the MES Ponnani College admissions portal to complete your institutional application.

03

Interview & Verification

Shortlisted candidates based on index marks will be called for document verification and interview at the campus.

04

Fee Payment & Confirm

Upon successful verification, secure your seat by paying the requisite semester fees to complete your admission process.
