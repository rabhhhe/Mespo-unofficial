About MES Ponnani

A Legacy of
Empowerment.

Established in 1968, MES Ponnani College remains a beacon of hope and advancement for the coastal regions of Malappuram district. Affiliated with the University of Calicut, the college was founded under the Muslim Educational Society (MES) with a visionary goal to provide inclusive and quality higher education to all.

Our Milestones

Five Decades
of Academic Growth

1968
The Foundation Stones

MES Ponnani College was established on a 25-acre lush green campus near the Ponnani estuary. Classes commenced under the patronage of the Muslim Educational Society, addressing a long-standing need for a center of higher education along the Malabar coastline.

1978
Expansion into Science & Commerce

The college expanded its initial arts curriculum to introduce major science streams and undergraduate programmes in commerce, building advanced laboratories and attracting scholars from across Kerala.

2004
NAAC Accreditation

Recognizing the institution's commitment to high teaching standards, research initiatives, and holistic community services, the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) accredited the college for the first time.

2018
Golden Jubilee & Modernization

Commemorating 50 years of academic service, the college underwent a massive infrastructure revitalization program, adding state-of-the-art computer labs, smart classrooms, and a highly automated central library.

Present
NAAC A+ & Technological Frontiers

With a prestigious NAAC A+ accreditation, MES Ponnani College stands at the forefront of modern education, introducing advanced programs like BSc in Artificial Intelligence and establishing active research cells in computer science, physics, and humanities.
